Luther Menard

The Co-founder of The UrbanCode.

I am an ios and web developer. I have worked in urban communities for half a decade trying to serve those that don't have the same opportunity and resources as those in a higher social-economic status. Compassion has lead me on a journey to provide opportunties for youth and adults to get into the tech world and learn exceptional skills to make them marketable as well as create leaders that will give back to the next generation for the glory of God. If you are compelled by this vision feel free to email me how you can get involved in serving the disenfranchised and disenherited in urban communities.
